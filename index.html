When Divorce Is a War Zone

Many divorces begin after a violent episode between the parties. If an arrest has been made for domestic violence, that case will start in General Sessions Criminal Court. If an order of protection has been sought, that case will often be filed in the General Sessions Civil Court. In most counties in Tennessee, your divorce will be filed in either Chancery or Circuit Court.

Some lawyers will only represent you in one of your cases and refer you to another lawyer for the rest. Venus Niner has experience as a prosecutor, a public defender, and as a divorce lawyer. She will represent you zealously, aggressively and competently, wherever your case leads.
